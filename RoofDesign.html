The Problem

Among different roof types of technologies and functions, there are debates and challenges regarding which one should be chosen and based on what criteria. In addition, Integrating different roof functions in a building also can improve the efficiency of the system and avoid single roof type deficiencies. The quation here is What are the best and/or optimal strategies of utilizing roof areas to achieve sustainability goals such as NZEBs or Carbon-Neutral buildings.

The Solution

Develop a user interface tool for sustainable roof design & development. RoofConsultant tool, is an interactive tool which focuses on roof design aspects different from the information that existing programs provided. This research provide qualitative information for every single technology and sustainable measures for designers and architects to consider.

Final Designs

It was important to cover every edge case when designing a product from the ground up.

Design Process

Once we determined the client needs for designing a sustaiable roof. I worked on the user flow of how the user might interact with the app.

Project Learnings

Learning about the user in the physical environment helped us understand the problem space better. The insights we uncovered in the research phase drove the design decisions of how the differet functinos would be integrated in the roof design and how it would interact with the roof consultant tool.
