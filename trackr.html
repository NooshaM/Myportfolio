Overview

The Problem

Travelers would like to hava an app experience that makes it easier to plan and book a trip. The app includes detailed information about booking hotels, flights, tour offerings as well as a booking form. Once a user’s plans are created and booked, what else can this product do to make the experience even better?

The Solution

It provides a user-friendly app experience for users, while evoking an inspiring sense of adventure. General product guidelines: - Enables the user to manage multiple itineraries - Provides a simple, intuitive UI where users can easily move from one stage of travel the next - A booking experience that builds the user’s confidence that they’re getting a good price/value and remaining within their budget - The overall experience enables users to feel in control, not overwhelmed, and feel like they’re discovering options (hotels, flights, food, activities) - Gives users the ability to customize and make changes to their plans.

Wireframing towards a solution

When tasked to redesign Trackr's website the first step was to iterate on ideas quickly. The best way to do that was by wireframing out the site and the message Trackr wanted to get across to potential customers.

Final Site Design

Trackr wanted to establish a web presence that would assert them as leaders in the tracking device space. It was important to showcase the sleek and stylish design of their Bravo device while showcasing relatable scenarios.

iOS App Design

Custom Icons
